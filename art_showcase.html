SGDA SUMMIT| 2022 ART SHOWCASE

OPEN CALL FOR SUBMISSIONS: To the Summit Art Show

Join us for the Student Game Developersâ€™ Alliance first virtual art gallery as part of our Summit 2022!

The artworks from this gallery will be showcased in web format during the SGDA Summit 2022, a student-targeted game developers conference with panels, talks, and a Student Games Showcase from June 26th - June 27th 2022 on Twitch. Art will be displayed on-stream and in a separate web-based gallery. The gallery will remain online after the event.

Artwork Criteria
  • Artwork must be your own creation or a close collaboration. All artists involved must be named.
  • Fanworks can be accepted.
  • Only 2D images can be accepted.
  • We will not accept artwork with hateful depictions and/or harmful ideologies.

Artist Criteria

  • You should either be a current student, recent alumni, or new/non-established within the games industry. This exhibition is a celebration of the work of emerging developers, artists, and creators.

Art Gallery

 The gallery is now open for anyone to play on the SGDA itch.io webpage! You can play it at the following link: https://sgda.itch.io/sgda-to-the-summit-art-gallery
