Global Game Jam 2021 | What It Is And Signups

The Global Game Jam is an international game jam hosted by a variety of organizations to encourage the development of games by everyone! In 48 hours, participants work in individual groups to create a game that fits around the theme of that jam. This year, GGJ is online, so you can participate in it from the comfort of your home and with people all over the globe.


Check out some past GGJ games!


This year, the Student Game Developers Alliance has it's own site for the jam! You must be concurrently enrolled in a university or within 3 years of graduation in order to sign up with us. The main site will be on Discord, where you can find a team or join with one already made!
