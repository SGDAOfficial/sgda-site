GAME JAM 2018 INFO

Crucial Feedback. Networking Opportunities. Constructive Workshops. Endless Ideas.

ABOUT

The SGDA game jam is a 48 hour event where student game developers get together to create awesome games! These games will be based off of a theme which will be announced at the day of the event. Our game jam will consist of workshops that will teach you the ins and outs of game development and professionals who will provide adequate feedback on your game. This is a great opportunity for those who want to collaborate with other fellow game developers to experiment on riveting new projects! This year we are collaborating with the Global Game Jam © and will be hosting two sites for Northern and Southern California.

WHAT WE OFFER

We are currently holding TWO sites that serve as spaces for you guys to work on your games in for both regions:

Northern California: UCSC

Southern California: UCR

If you're unfamiliar with game development, fret not! We will have workshops for during the first day to kickstart yourselves into making games.
