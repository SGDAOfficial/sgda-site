SGDA Summit 2021


Summit Info


The fifth SGDA Summit 2021 is taking place Saturday May 29th - Sunday May 30th. Join us online in this free, student-targeted games conference with workshops, guest speakers, and a student games showcase to celebrate some of the best emerging talent in the games industry. Click below to join the Discord!

Be a part of SGDA

SGDA is always looking for extra hands. Want to help with Summit preparations, give feedback on how our organization can move forward, or have ideas for what SGDA can do for growing game devs and how to take action? Reach out at contact@sgda.io or join the SGDA Discord and talk to some of the officers there!

Funding

You can also sponsor the SGDA Summit or send us a donation! Our new funding goals this year include honorarium for our guest speakers, grants for the student devs in our showcase, and financing the SGDA website and organization upkeep. With your support, we hope to give back to devs more than ever this year.
